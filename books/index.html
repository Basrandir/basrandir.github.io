Books

There was a period of time in my life where I used to read a lot. I’d regularly go through 3 or 4 books a month. Unfortunately that time is long passed and I’m now lucky if I get through 3 or 4 books a year. This is my attempt to boost that number by cataloging what I’ve read and potentially keeping notes on them as well. I will also try to add to this all the books I’ve ever read as accurately as possible.
